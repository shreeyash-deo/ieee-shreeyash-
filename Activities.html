Activities

Research Club

You have an idea, we have a platform. IEEE as a global organization has been successful in uplifting the research quality of the students and elevating their innovative skills. With a global network of researchers and engineers, our club provides an unparalleled platform for collaboration and knowledge sharing through paper reading sessions and industrial visits.

Coding Club

Our mission is to create a fun and supportive environment where we develop programming and analytical skills together. The main vision of the club is to improve competitive programming skills in students and prepare them for renowned competitions like ACM-ICPC, Google Code Jam, and national & international hackathons.
